About Us

With an average of more than 31 million visits each month, Cars.com is the leading online destination for car shoppers and owners. We offer credible and easy-to-understand information to help consumers research, price and find new and used vehicles and quality service and repair providers.

Through unbiased editorial content, vehicle listings, dealer reviews, side-by-side comparison options, build and price tools, photo galleries and videos, Cars.com gives drivers the information they need to make confident decisions. Launched in June 1998, Cars.com is owned by TEGNA Inc. and operates Auto.com, NewCars.com® and PickupTrucks.com™.
